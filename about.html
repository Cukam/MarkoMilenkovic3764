ABOUT ME

I am a graffiti artist from Serbia. I live in Nis, the largest city in the south of the country.
I started drawing graffiti around 2010. In the beginning, it was not serious and pointless painting.
I had a big break in painting graffiti during school.
Three years ago, in 2018, I started painting graffiti again, but much more seriously than in all previous years. I like to paint graffiti on apartment buildings that do not have a decorated facade.
